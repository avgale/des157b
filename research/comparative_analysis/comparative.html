Comparative Analysis

The Hottest Year

This project follows a chronological narrative, and present data step-by-step over time. As a viewer, this format is easy to digest, and the scroll boxes allow me to view all the data at the same time as the captions without having to go back and forth between them. The use of specific examples, like the street temparatures in Phoenix show the micro scale, while the early statistics present a macro scale.

Journey into Sleep

I really like the illustrative style of this project. The scroll format which changes small elements of a consistent image helps the viewer focus on the aspect being highlighted by the caption. The switching off between scroll interactions and blocks of text with graphs provides variety and holds the viewer's attention.
